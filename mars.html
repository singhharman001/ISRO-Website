About Satellite Communication

Satellite communication is a technology that enables wireless transmission of data, voice, and video via satellites orbiting the Earth.

How It Works

Signals are transmitted from ground stations to satellites and then relayed back to receivers on Earth. This enables long-distance communication.

Applications

  • Telecommunication
  • Broadcasting
  • Weather Forecasting
  • Navigation
  • Military and Defense
